Final Project

My partner and I decided to make a version of the game air hockey. The goal of the game is to get as many points as you
can to 10, BEFORE the other person does. It works like pong in that way but a bit more complicated in the structure of the board
it is on.

To start, you click "Start" and choose which player you would like to be. For player one, use the left and right keys
to move your paddle left and right. For player 2 do the same with the ctrl and alt keys. When one of you hits 7 points, the air
grid will change color and the ball will begin to speed up to make the game more challenging.
